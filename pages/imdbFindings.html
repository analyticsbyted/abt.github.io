IMDb Movies and Shows Findings

The analysis project focuses on exploring and visualizing the IMDb movies dataset sourced from Kaggle. The dataset, which can be found at: Kaggle IMDb Dataset, provides information about the top 1000 movies and TV shows on IMDb.

The goal of the project was to gain insights into various aspects of the dataset using Tableau reporting tool. By leveraging the provided dataset, the analysis project aimed to provide a comprehensive understanding of the top 1000 movies and TV shows based on IMDb ratings.

The analysis project included several key visualizations and KPI cards that provided valuable information about the movies. Users could select a specific movie title from a dropdown menu, which dynamically filtered the KPI cards to display relevant information about the selected movie. This information encompassed details such as the year of release, genre, duration, IMDb rating, number of votes received, gross sales, meta score, and a film description.

Furthermore, the analysis project utilized Tableau to create insightful visualizations that shed light on the dataset as a whole. These visualizations included:

  1. Top 10 Movies by Vote: Showcasing the movies with the highest number of votes, with "The Shawshank Redemption" leading the pack with 2,343,110 votes.
  2. Maturity Ratings by Movies: Displaying the distribution of maturity ratings among the movies, highlighting the prevalence of certain ratings such as 'U' and 'A'.
  3. Time Series Area Chart for Gross Sales by Year: Illustrating the trend of gross sales over the years, with a notable peak in 2009 at $2.9 billion. The chart also revealed significant decreases in sales during 2011 and 2013.
  4. Time Series Area Chart for Total Movies by Release Year: Visualizing the trend of movie releases over time, indicating a general upward trend with a peak in 2014, followed by a decline in subsequent years.
  5. Word Cloud Chart of Genres: Presenting a word cloud of genres, highlighting the prevalence of genres such as Drama, Action, Crime, and others related to Drama.
  6. Box Plots of IMDb Ratings by Top 10 Genres: Examining IMDb ratings for the top 10 genres through box plots, identifying outliers such as "The Godfather" with a rating of 9.2 in the Crime, Drama genre.
  7. Box Plots of Meta Score by Top 10 Genres: Analyzing meta scores for the top 10 genres through box plots, showcasing outliers such as "7 Pounds" and "I am Sam" in the Drama category with scores below the lower whisker.

By leveraging the IMDb movies dataset from Kaggle, this analysis project provided a comprehensive exploration of the top 1000 movies and TV shows, aiming to uncover valuable insights and trends within the dataset.
